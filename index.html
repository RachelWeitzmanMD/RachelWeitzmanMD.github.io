Dr. Weitzman is a Harvard-educated surgeon specializing in facial rejuvenation, with a focus on deep plane facelift, rhinoplasty, and the aging face. She brings a surgeon-scientist's perspective to every patient — grounding aesthetic decisions in anatomy, evidence, and precision.

She is currently completing her residency in Otolaryngology–Head & Neck Surgery at NewYork-Presbyterian / Columbia and Weill Cornell, and will be joining NYU as a Facial Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery fellow in July 2026.
